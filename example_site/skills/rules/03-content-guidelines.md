# Content Guidelines: Banish the "AI Slop"

Your goal is to write copy that feels human, punchy, and valuable. Avoid the generic "corporate speak" that LLMs default to.

## The "Never Use" List
Do not use these words or phrases. They are instant markers of low-effort AI generation:
- "Unlock" (e.g., "Unlock your potential")
- "Elevate" (e.g., "Elevate your business")
- "In today's fast-paced digital world..."
- "Game-changer"
- "Revolutionary"
- "Seamlessly"
- "Cutting-edge"
- "Robust"
- "Tapestry"

## The "Do This Instead" List
1.  **Be Specific:** Instead of "Maximize efficiency", say "Save 2 hours per day".
2.  **Focus on "You":** Talk to the reader, not about the product.
3.  **Use Active Verbs:** "We build websites" is better than "Website building services are provided by us".
4.  **Short Sentences:** Punchy. Direct. Easy to read.
5.  **Address Pain Points:** Start with the problem the user has, then offer the solution.

## Tone of Voice
- **Confident but Humble:** efficient, professional, not arrogant.
- **Clear:** No jargon unless strictly necessary for the audience.
- **Relatable:** Write like a smart human talking to another smart human.
