# Ideal Customer Profile — НИКЕ Hair Salon

## Demographics
- **Age:** 22–45
- **Gender:** Predominantly male (70%), growing female clientele (30%)
- **Income:** Upper-middle to high; values quality over price
- **Location:** Saint Petersburg, Russia — within 30-minute travel to Nevsky Prospekt area
- **Occupation:** Professionals, entrepreneurs, creative industry workers, IT sector

## Main Problems
1. **Low-quality service** — inconsistent results at mass-market salons
2. **No personalization** — generic haircuts that don't match face shape or lifestyle
3. **Uncomfortable atmosphere** — crowded, noisy, impersonal environments
4. **Time wasted** — long wait times, no online booking, poor scheduling
5. **Post-visit dissatisfaction** — style doesn't hold, no maintenance guidance

## Why They Choose НИКЕ
- **Master-level stylists** with verifiable portfolio and training credentials
- **Premium experience** — curated interior, personal attention, no rushing
- **Predictable excellence** — every visit feels like the first time, consistently high results
- **Convenient booking** — fast online reservation, minimal waiting
- **Full grooming ecosystem** — haircuts, beard, facial care all under one roof

| Trigger | How We Activate It |

## Decision Journey
1. **Sees ad / recommendation** → Instagram, Google Maps, referral
2. **Checks reviews + portfolio** → Social proof is decisive
3. **Visits website** → Must feel premium, warm, and trustworthy within 3 seconds
4. **Books or calls** → CTA must be immediately visible and frictionless
5. **First visit** → Experience must exceed expectations → repeat client
