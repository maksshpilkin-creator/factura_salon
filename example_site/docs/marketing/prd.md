# Product Requirements Document — НИКЕ Hair Salon Website

## Website Purpose
A high-converting, mobile-first single-page landing site for НИКЕ Hair Salon (Saint Petersburg). The site must:
- Instantly communicate premium quality and warm atmosphere
- Convert visitors into booking leads (call, WhatsApp, contact form)
- Build trust through social proof, visual portfolio, and transparent pricing
- Rank in local search (SEO-optimized)

## Conversion Goals
| Priority | Goal | Metric |
|----------|------|--------|
| 🥇 Primary | Phone call / WhatsApp message | Click-to-call, click-to-chat |
| 🥈 Secondary | Contact form submission | Form completions |
| 🥉 Tertiary | Location visit (map directions) | Map click-through |

**Target:** 5–8% visitor-to-lead conversion rate.

## Landing Page Sections

### 1. Hero
- Full-viewport, warm-toned hero with brand name, tagline, and primary CTA
- Background image of salon interior or styling work

### 2. Services
- Card grid showcasing key services with icons, brief descriptions, and price ranges
- Visual hierarchy: most popular service highlighted

### 3. About
- Brand story, mission statement, and team introduction
- Interior photo or stylist portrait

### 4. Advantages
- 4–6 advantage cards: experience, quality products, atmosphere, results guarantee
- Icons + short copy per card

### 5. Reviews
- Client testimonial carousel/cards with names and ratings
- Star ratings for trust signals

### 6. Gallery
- Before/after or interior photo grid
- Lightbox interaction for full-resolution viewing

### 7. Call-to-Action
- Large, dedicated CTA section with contrasting background
- Phone + WhatsApp + Form options

### 8. Contact
- Contact form (name, phone, message)
- Business hours, phone number, WhatsApp link

### 9. Map
- Embedded Google Map or styled map image
- Address and directions

### 10. Footer
- Brand info, navigation links, social media, legal

## Key Features
- Sticky navigation with scroll-aware highlighting
- Smooth scroll between sections
- Mobile hamburger menu
- Floating phone/WhatsApp buttons
- Scroll-triggered fade-in animations
- Contact form with validation
- SEO meta tags (title, description, Open Graph)
