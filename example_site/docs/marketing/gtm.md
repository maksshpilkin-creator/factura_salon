# Go-To-Market Strategy — НИКЕ Hair Salon

## How the Business Gets Clients

### Channel Mix
| Channel | Role | Priority |
|---------|------|----------|
| **Instagram** | Portfolio showcase, stories, Reels | 🥇 Primary |
| **Google Maps / Yandex Maps** | Local search discoverability | 🥇 Primary |
| **Word of Mouth** | Referral program, loyalty clients | 🥈 Secondary |
| **Website (this project)** | Central landing page for all traffic | 🥇 Primary |
| **Telegram** | Community, promos, booking reminders | 🥈 Secondary |
| **VK** | Regional social presence | 🥉 Tertiary |

### Client Acquisition Funnel
1. **Discovery** — Instagram ad, Google search "барбершоп Петербург", referral
2. **Evaluation** — Lands on website → sees portfolio, reviews, prices
3. **Decision** — Clicks CTA (call/WhatsApp/form)
4. **Booking** — Confirms appointment
5. **Retention** — Follow-up, loyalty program, re-booking prompts

## Positioning

### Brand Promise
> "Мы не просто стрижем — мы создаем архитектуру вашего стиля."
> (We don't just cut hair — we architect your style.)

### Competitive Differentiators
- **Named masters** — you know who will cut your hair before booking
- **Consultation-first** — every visit starts with understanding your goals
- **Premium atmosphere** — warm, quiet, personal (not a factory assembly line)
- **Full grooming** — hair + beard + facial care in one premium visit

### Market Position
Mid-to-premium segment. Not the cheapest, never the most corporate. Positioned as the "personal stylist" experience accessible without club memberships.

## Trust Signals (must appear on the website)
- ⭐ Client reviews with real names and star ratings
- 📸 Real before/after photos (not stock)
- 🏆 Stylist credentials and years of experience
- 📍 Verified Google Maps listing with 4.8+ rating
- 📞 Visible phone number and WhatsApp (no hiding behind forms)
- 🔒 "1000+ довольных клиентов" social proof counter
