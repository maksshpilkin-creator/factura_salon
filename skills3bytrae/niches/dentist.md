# Dentist Niche Guide

## Design Aesthetics
-   **Vibe**: Trustworthy, Clean, Professional, Modern, High-Tech.
-   **Colors**:
    -   `--color-primary`: Navy Blue (#003366), Teal (#008080), or Sky Blue (#00BFFF).
    -   `--color-bg`: Pure White (#FFFFFF).
    -   `--font-heading`: Clean Sans-serif (e.g., Montserrat).
    -   `--font-body`: Readable Sans-serif (e.g., Open Sans).

## Key Sections
1.  **Hero**: Smiling patient or friendly doctor.
2.  **Services**: Icons for General, Cosmetic, Orthodontics.
3.  **Insurance/Payment**: Logos of accepted insurance plans.
4.  **Testimonials**: "Pain-free" is the keyword to highlight.
5.  **Emergency**: "Same Day Appointments" banner.

## Copywriting Examples
-   **H1**: "Your Smile, Our Passion. Best Dentist in [City]."
-   **H2**: "Gentle, Affordable Dental Care for the Whole Family"
-   **CTA**: "Schedule Your Exam"

## Specific Features
-   **New Patient Special**: Coupon or offer ($99 Cleaning & Exam).
-   **Online Forms**: Link to patient portal.
