# Consulting / Professional Services Niche Guide

## Design Aesthetics
-   **Vibe**: Corporate, Reliable, Expert, Serious.
-   **Colors**:
    -   `--color-primary`: Navy Blue (#000080), Charcoal (#36454F), or Burgundy (#800020).
    -   `--color-bg`: White (#FFFFFF).
    -   `--font-heading`: Classic Serif (e.g., Merriweather) or Clean Sans (e.g., Helvetica).
    -   `--font-body`: Serif (e.g., Georgia) for readability.

## Key Sections
1.  **Hero**: Professional headshot or skyline.
2.  **Services**: Grid of expertise areas.
3.  **Case Studies**: "How we helped X achieve Y".
4.  **Testimonials**: LinkedIn style recommendations.
5.  **Lead Magnet**: "Download our Whitepaper".

## Copywriting Examples
-   **H1**: "Strategic [Service] for Growing Businesses in [City]"
-   **H2**: "Data-Driven Solutions. Proven Results."
-   **CTA**: "Book a Free Consultation"

## Specific Features
-   **Calendly Embed**: For booking calls.
-   **Blog/Insights**: To demonstrate authority.
