# Fitness Studio / Gym Niche Guide

## Design Aesthetics
-   **Vibe**: Energetic, Strong, Motivating, Community.
-   **Colors**:
    -   `--color-primary`: Neon Green (#39FF14), Electric Blue (#00FFFF), or Bold Red (#FF0000).
    -   `--color-bg`: Dark/Black (#000000) or Concrete Grey.
    -   `--font-heading`: Bold, Condensed Sans-serif (e.g., Bebas Neue).
    -   `--font-body`: Roboto or Inter.

## Key Sections
1.  **Hero**: Action shot (lifting, running, yoga pose).
2.  **Classes/Schedule**: Weekly calendar view.
3.  **Trainers**: Bios and specialties.
4.  **Membership**: Pricing tables (Drop-in vs Monthly).
5.  **Free Pass**: Lead magnet form.

## Copywriting Examples
-   **H1**: "Transform Your Body, Transform Your Life at [Gym Name]"
-   **H2**: "High-Intensity Training for All Fitness Levels"
-   **CTA**: "Get Your Free 7-Day Pass"

## Specific Features
-   **Class Booking**: MindBody integration or similar.
-   **Success Stories**: Before/After photos.
