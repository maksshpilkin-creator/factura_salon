# Local Business Pattern

A balanced approach for establishing long-term trust and brand presence.

## Section Order

1.  **Header**: Full nav with "About", "Services", "Contact".
2.  **Hero**: Friendly, welcoming image.
3.  **Intro/About**: "Serving [City] since [Year]". Photo of the owner.
4.  **Detailed Services**: Grid of 3-6 services with descriptions.
5.  **Map/Location**: CRITICAL. Show where you are.
6.  **Hours of Operation**: Clear table.
7.  **Reviews**: Google Maps embed or testimonial slider.
8.  **Contact Form**: General inquiry.
9.  **Footer**: Full NAP and social links.

## Usage
Best for: Restaurants, Retail, Dentists, Salons, Law Firms.
