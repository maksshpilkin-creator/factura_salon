# Content Writing Guidelines

Text content should feel:

editorial  
minimal  
luxury  
cinematic  

Avoid marketing language.

---

# Forbidden Words

Avoid:

- best solution
- cutting-edge
- revolutionary
- mission critical
- seamless experience

These are typical AI phrases.

---

# Typography Hierarchy

Structure text using clear hierarchy.

Example:

Small Label  
Large Title  
Short Subtitle  

Example:

EXPLORE THE COLLECTION

The Art of Motion

A curated archive of cinematic work.

---

# Paragraph Rules

Prefer:

short paragraphs  
clean spacing  
clear hierarchy

Avoid long blocks of text.
