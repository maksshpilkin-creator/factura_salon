# Lead Generation Pattern

Focuses aggressively on capturing the lead (email/phone) above the fold.

## Section Order

1.  **Header**: Minimal. Logo and Phone Number only. No distraction links.
2.  **Hero**:
    -   Headline: High Promise.
    -   **Form**: Embedded DIRECTLY in the hero (not just a button).
    -   Form Fields: Name, Phone, "Get Quote".
3.  **Trust Indicators**: "Guaranteed Response in 10 mins".
4.  **Benefits**: Bullet points of why they should submit.
5.  **Testimonials**: To reassure before scrolling.
6.  **How it Works**: 3 simple steps (Submit -> We Call -> Job Done).
7.  **Footer**: Minimal. Privacy Policy, Terms.

## Usage
Best for: Emergency services (Plumbers, Towing), Real Estate, Insurance.
