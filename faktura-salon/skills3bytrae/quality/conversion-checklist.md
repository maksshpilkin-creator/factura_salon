# Conversion Optimization (CRO) Checklist

Ensure the page is designed to convert visitors into leads.

## Above the Fold
- [ ] **Clear Value Prop**: Does the H1 say *exactly* what is offered?
- [ ] **Visible CTA**: Is there a button immediately visible without scrolling?
- [ ] **Contact Info**: Is the phone number clickable in the header?

## Trust Elements
- [ ] **Social Proof**: Are testimonials real and specific?
- [ ] **Authority**: Are licenses/certifications displayed?
- [ ] **Guarantee**: Is there a risk-reversal (e.g., "Satisfaction Guaranteed")?

## Friction Removal
- [ ] **Forms**: Are there fewer than 5 fields?
- [ ] **Speed**: Does the page load instantly?
- [ ] **Mobile**: Are buttons easy to tap with a thumb?

## Call to Action
- [ ] **Contrast**: Do buttons stand out from the background?
- [ ] **Action Verbs**: Use "Get Quote" instead of "Submit".
