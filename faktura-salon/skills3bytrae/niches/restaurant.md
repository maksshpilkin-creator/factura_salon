# Restaurant Niche Guide

## Design Aesthetics
-   **Vibe**: Appetizing, Warm, Atmospheric, bustling.
-   **Colors**:
    -   `--color-primary`: Deep Red (#8B0000), Burnt Orange (#CC5500), or Slate (#2F4F4F).
    -   `--color-bg`: Dark mode often works well (#1A1A1A) or Warm Beige (#F5F5DC).
    -   `--font-heading`: Display font (e.g., Oswald or a Script font).
    -   `--font-body`: Readable Serif or Sans.

## Key Sections
1.  **Hero**: Full-width video or mouth-watering food photography.
2.  **Menu**: PDF download AND HTML text version (for SEO).
3.  **Reservations**: OpenTable/Resy integration or simple form.
4.  **About**: Chef's story or farm-to-table philosophy.
5.  **Location/Hours**: Very prominent.

## Copywriting Examples
-   **H1**: "Authentic [Cuisine] Flavors in the Heart of [City]"
-   **H2**: "Farm-Fresh Ingredients, Unforgettable Taste"
-   **CTA**: "Reserve Your Table"

## Specific Features
-   **Online Ordering**: Link to UberEats/DoorDash or internal system.
-   **Gallery**: Food porn images.
