# Beauty Salon Niche Guide

## Design Aesthetics
-   **Vibe**: Luxury, Calm, Clean, Pampering.
-   **Colors**:
    -   `--color-primary`: Gold (#D4AF37), Soft Pink (#F4C2C2), or Sage Green (#9DC183).
    -   `--color-bg`: White (#FFFFFF) or Very Light Grey (#FAFAFA).
    -   `--font-heading`: Serif font (e.g., Playfair Display).
    -   `--font-body`: Sans-serif (e.g., Lato or Inter).

## Key Sections
1.  **Hero**: High-quality image of a relaxed client or beautiful interior.
2.  **Service Menu**: Price list is crucial. Use a clean table or list layout.
3.  **Gallery**: Instagram-style grid of results (hair, nails, makeup).
4.  **Team**: Stylist profiles with photos.
5.  **Booking**: "Book Now" sticky button.

## Copywriting Examples
-   **H1**: "Reveal Your True Radiance in [City]"
-   **H2**: "Luxury Hair & Spa Services Tailored to You"
-   **CTA**: "Book Your Relaxation Session"

## Specific Features
-   **Instagram Feed Integration**: Essential for social proof.
-   **Gift Card Purchase**: prominent link.
